The Site is Live!

March 2026 · Andres Gamez

Welcome to my new website! Since 2024 I have managed a personal website to act as a portfolio and repository for my work and accomplishments. My old site was feeling a bit dated and since then I've learned a lot about coding and site design, so with all those skills I decided to launch this new site.

The site has a few moving parts. The Projects page is the main event — it's organized into signature work, collections, and professional collaborations. These projects span work I've done in classes, through funded research, during internships, and semester capstone projects.

A few things I'm especially proud of: the Denver RTD Transit Station Explorer — a web app I built with the ArcGIS Maps SDK that lets you filter and query the light rail and commuter rail network interactively, and the TCU Neighborhood Commercial District StoryMap, which came out of field survey work I did right around campus.

I'm finishing up my dual degrees in History and Geography at TCU in May and looking for opportunities to do this kind of work professionally. If something here catches your eye, I'd love to hear from you. Reach out and let's work together.

Happy exploring the site!
