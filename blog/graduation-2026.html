Graduating May 2026

March 2026 · Andres Gamez

After four years at Texas Christian University, I'm finishing up my final semester and walking across the stage on May 9, 2026. I'll be receiving a Bachelor of Arts in History and a Bachelor of Science in Geography — two degrees for the price of one!

My majors have shaped my time so much at TCU. I started as a History major, picked up a Geography minor my freshman year, then upgraded it each successive year. I went from a Geography Minor, to a double major, to a dual degree.

I'm proud of the work I've done here — the internships, the coursework, the things I've made and the ones I'm still working on. But more than anything, I'm excited about what comes next.
