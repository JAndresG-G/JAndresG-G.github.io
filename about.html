About

Hey, I'm Andres.

I'm a Senior at Texas Christian University finishing dual degrees in History and Geography, expecting to graduate in May 2026. I'm eager to translate my academic development into a GIS Analyst role focused on cartographic design.

I'm proficient in the Esri ArcGIS suite — with extensive experience in ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, and Esri apps — as well as code-driven data visualization tools such as Google Colab with Python, Cursor, and Claude Code. My academic concentration has been on GIS visualizations and cartographic design.

During internships with the TCU Center for Urban Studies and the North Central Texas Council of Governments, I assisted with database management, project support, and ArcGIS solutions development.

I'm a driven team member ready to grow in a professional setting where I can apply the breadth of my education to support strong results for a mission-driven organization.
