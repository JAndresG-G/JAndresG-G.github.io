Signature Projects

TCU Neighborhood Commercial District Analysis

↗

Montserrat Mountains Map

↗

A stylistic cartographic final project representing the Montserrat Mountains in Catalonia, Spain. Inspired by Eduard Imhof's shaded relief techniques, rendered using Esri Living Atlas and OpenStreetMap data.

ArcGIS Pro Shaded Relief Cartographic Design

Denver RTD Transit Station Explorer

↗

An interactive ArcGIS Maps SDK web application mapping the Denver RTD light rail and commuter rail network — with live feature querying, line-based symbology, sidebar filtering by status and parking, and styled station popups.

ArcGIS Maps SDK JavaScript Transit Denver

Professional Research Collaborations

Work produced in professional settings during internships and research appointments.

North Texas Sanborn Historic Map Database

Research Assistant — TCU Center for Urban Studies

Spatial analysis and georeferencing of 800+ historical Sanborn Insurance maps using ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Online, building a usable spatial dataset of historic maps of the North Texas region to support historic and geographic research.

ArcGIS Pro Georeferencing Historical GIS TCU CUS

Texas Zoning & Housing Development Analysis (SB 840)

Research Assistant — TCU Center for Urban Studies

Collaborated with the Center for Urban Studies to research and develop a spatial analysis that models and projects the effects of Texas Senate Bill 840, expanding the range of construction for multifamily and mixed-use development across 15 cities in Texas.

ArcGIS Pro Spatial Analysis Urban Policy TCU CUS
