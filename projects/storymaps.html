StoryMaps

A collection of ArcGIS StoryMaps — each tile links directly to the published story.

Samuel Guy Inman — Biographical Portfolio

↗

An interactive biographical portfolio documenting the life and career of Dr. Samuel Guy Inman, American diplomat, educator, and statesman. Drawing on archival materials and primary sources, the StoryMap traces Inman's contributions to U.S.-Latin American relations and his work in international education and diplomacy.

Biography Latin America Historical Geography Digital Humanities

TCU Neighborhood Commercial District Analysis

↗

An analysis of the economic activity of businesses surrounding the intersection of University St. and West Berry St. — examining business clustering, market relationships, and student spending patterns within a 0.25-mile buffer of the TCU campus using ArcGIS Survey123 field data.

Economic Geography ArcGIS Survey123 Fort Worth TCU

Garmin Vívoactive 6 — Global Supply Chain Analysis

↗

Tracing the production of Garmin's Vívoactive 6 smartwatch from raw material extraction to finished product — mapping a global supply chain across Taiwan, South Korea, Japan, China, Chile, Australia, and the DRC, and examining the labor conditions, environmental impact, and uneven development embedded in a single consumer device.

Supply Chain Economic Geography Global Production

H-E-B DFW Locations Analysis

↗

A spatial analysis of H-E-B grocery store locations across the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, examining the retailer's site selection patterns, market coverage, and expansion strategy as it enters one of Texas's most competitive grocery markets.

Retail Geography Site Selection Dallas–Fort Worth

Consumption Analysis — 7 Days of Spending

↗

A spatial analysis of personal spending patterns over seven consecutive days, mapping consumption activity by location, category, and time. The StoryMap examines the geography of everyday spending and what it reveals about urban mobility and consumer behavior.
