Final Project

Montserrat Mountains Map

A cartographic representation of the Parc Natural de la Muntanya de Montserrat in Catalonia, Spain — inspired by Eduard Imhof's shaded relief techniques and built with custom models in ArcGIS Pro.

Lab 1 — Map 1

Lab 1 — Map 2

Lab 2 — Thematic Mapping

Intercounty Infrastructural Development

Using Lab 1's dataset, a thematic approach was applied here to communicate spatial data patterns with a clean and pleasing design.

Lab 3 — Graduated & Dot Density Symbols

Virginia Household on Public Assistance Income: Graduated Symbol

A comparative analysis of representations of point based data. This map utilized graduated symbols to visualize the number of of Virginian households on public assistance income across three scales.

Virginia Household on Public Assistance Income: Dot Density Symbol

A comparative analysis of representations of point based data. This map utilized dot density aggregation to visualize the number of of Virginian households on public assistance income across three scales.

A pair of maps visualizing uninsurance rate by education attainment in New England between two different groups visualized with a graduated color scheme.

Uninsured Rate by Education Attainment: Bivariate Scheme

A pair of maps visualizing uninsurance rate by education attainment in New England between two different groups visualized with a bivariate color scheme.

Uninsurance Rate: Non-High School Graduates Over 25

A pair of maps visualizing uninsurance rate of Non-High School graduates over 25 in New England visualized between Graduated vs. Classed Scheme.

Lab 5 — GIS for Marketing

Heathrow: The Heart of Aviation

Marketing material map designed to show the centrality of London Heathrow Airport to international flights by showing routine flights to and from LHR by their flight length in kilometers.

Heathrow: Fly at any Hour

Promotional material developed to show the amount of flights offered throughout each time of day to and from London Heathrow Airport, showing that LHR's timings can accommodate any flyer's needs.

Heathrow: Head to your Dream Destination

Promotional material for London Heathrow Airport showing the density of flights to and from the most visited nations.

Lab 6 — Terrain & Elevation Visualization

Paradise Valley Trail Run 2025

This map was developed as a guide for runners for the fictional Paradise Valley Trail Run. The map is intended as a reference pamphlet for runners to understand the course routes, elevation change, and stations.

Lab 7 — Advanced Layout & Cartographic Refinement

National Social Support

This global map shows the grade of each nation's national social support networks with an uncertainty score applied for data transparency.

Perceptions of Corruption

This layout uses a graduated color scheme to show the perceived level of corruption for Latin American nations.

Perceptions of Freedom

This map layout uses a bivariate color scheme to show the perceived freedom of Latin American nations.

National Life Ladder Placement

This map layout uses a graduated color scheme to show the placement of each Latin American nation on the Cantril Life Ladder.

Lab 9 — Physical Output & Laser Engraving

Jalostotitlán, Jalisco (Digital)

A hand referenced digital layout map of Jalostotitlán, Jalisco, Mexico. The digital version was created to laser engrave into a piece of wood to create a physical map product.

Jalostotitlán, Jalisco (Woodcut)

A hand referenced woodcut layout map of Jalostotitlán, Jalisco, Mexico. This woodcut version of the map was laser engraved based on the digital model.
